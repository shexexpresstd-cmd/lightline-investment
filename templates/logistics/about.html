About Lightline Logistics

The trusted bridge connecting Chinese suppliers to East African markets.

Who We Are

China to East Africa
Your Logistics Bridge

Lightline Logistics is a dedicated freight forwarding and logistics company specializing in the China to East Africa trade corridor. We provide end-to-end container cargo solutions — from factory sourcing in China to final delivery across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and DRC.

With offices in Nairobi and partner operations in Shanghai and Shenzhen, we speak the language of trade on both ends. Our team handles every detail: supplier coordination, cargo consolidation, sea freight, customs clearance, warehousing, and last-mile delivery.
