Who We Are

About
Light Line Investment

Light Line Investment Ltd is a holding company founded in 2010 by highly experienced and skilled entrepreneurs. It is a registered company both in Kenya and Somalia. The major activities of the company are general services, construction, trade, and investments.

The main purpose of Light Line is to invest in viable business opportunities where all stakeholders get involved, benefit, and deliver services and goods of the best possible standards.

Light Line Investment Ltd is a dynamic and competitive company whose superior market strategy is indisputable and quite remarkable due to its exceptional performance with developed business guidelines and policies that are of international standards. It has supplied thousands of quality products, rendered satisfactory services to its esteemed customers, and implemented various projects successfully.
