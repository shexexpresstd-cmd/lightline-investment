Who We Are

A Diversified
Holding Company

Lightline Investment Group is the parent company of a family of specialized businesses. We provide strategic direction, capital, and operational support to our subsidiaries, enabling them to excel in their respective markets.

Our portfolio spans five key sectors: engineering, logistics, capital management, research and development, and professional training. Each subsidiary operates with operational independence while benefiting from the group's shared resources, network, and strategic vision.

Together, we employ over 500 professionals and serve clients across the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Europe.
